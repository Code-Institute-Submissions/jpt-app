Meet James

Hi everyone, my name is James Green and I have been Personal Training for 10 years now and have a wealth of experience and knowledge

I combine my boxing and conditioning knowledge with weight and strength training to give you a great blend and range of exercise plans to combat any goal.

I have 6 years training and competing as an Amateur Boxing during my teens and early twenties, where I built my conditioning knowledge. During this time I was also competing on track and distance running, often running 1,500, 3k and 5k at club level. I then went on to run in two half marathons, so know what is required for distance running.

In my late twenties I turned to body building, comprising of 8 years strength and weight training, finally competing in physique competitions during 2016.

I am happy to train in any location and have the knowledge and materials required to adapt to home or studio sessions. Lets get stared!
